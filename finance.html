What You Get
Finance Lead Types

Specialized lead products designed for every segment of the finance market.

Debt Relief & Settlement

Consumers with $10K–$100K+ in unsecured debt actively seeking consolidation, settlement, or management programs. Includes debt amount, types, and hardship reason.

Personal Loans

Borrowers searching for personal loan offers, pre-qualification, and rate comparisons. Includes requested amount, credit range, and purpose of loan.

Credit Repair

Consumers looking to improve their credit score, dispute negative items, and rebuild their credit profile. Includes current score range and goals.

Mortgage & Refinance

Homeowners exploring refinancing for rate reduction, cash-out, or term change. Also first-time homebuyer leads with pre-approval interest.

Tax Relief

Consumers with IRS or state tax debt seeking resolution, offer-in-compromise, or installment agreement assistance.

Student Loan

Borrowers seeking refinancing, consolidation, or forgiveness program information for federal and private student loans.

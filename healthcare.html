What You Get
Healthcare Lead Types

Specialized lead products designed for every segment of the healthcare market.

Medicare Advantage

Turning-65 and aging-in leads, plan comparison shoppers, and beneficiaries seeking plan changes during AEP and OEP. Age-verified with current coverage details.

Medicare Supplement

Beneficiaries exploring Medigap plans for out-of-pocket cost protection. Includes current Plan letter, premium, and reason for shopping.

ACA Marketplace

Individuals and families seeking health insurance through the marketplace during open and special enrollment. Income-verified for subsidy eligibility.

Dental, Vision & Hearing

Consumers shopping for supplemental coverage plans. Particularly strong demand among Medicare-eligible populations.

Rehab & Treatment

Individuals and families seeking addiction treatment, behavioral health, and rehabilitation services. Includes insurance verification and clinical pre-screening.

Final Expense

Seniors seeking affordable burial and final expense insurance. Age-verified with health screening questions and coverage amount preferences.

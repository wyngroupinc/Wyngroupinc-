What You Get
Insurance Lead Types

Specialized lead products designed for every segment of the insurance market.

Auto Insurance

Drivers comparing rates due to rate increases, new vehicles, moving, or policy renewal. Includes vehicle info, current premium, and driving history.

Homeowners Insurance

Homeowners shopping for coverage due to refinancing, new purchase, switching providers, or rate increases. Property details included.

Life Insurance

Consumers exploring term, whole, and universal life options triggered by life events — marriage, new baby, mortgage, retirement planning.

Health & ACA

Individuals and families seeking marketplace coverage during open enrollment and special enrollment periods. Includes income and household size.

Medicare

Turning-65, Medicare Advantage, Medigap, and Part D leads year-round. Age-verified with current coverage and eligibility status.

Commercial Lines

Small business owners seeking general liability, workers comp, professional liability, and commercial auto coverage.

Consent

Real-time API, ping/post, portal download, email, CRM push (Salesforce, AgencyBloc, HawkSoft)

Filters

Volume

Scalable from 10 to 1,000+ leads per day across all lines

Returns
